<h1>Rules</h1>
<h3>Welcome To The Memory Game</h3>
<p>The game has 12 cards consisting of 6 pairs. You have to click on two cards in a row.<br>Clicking on two similar cards will result in a match.<br> An unsuccessfull match will result in both the cards turning back.</p>

<h3>Remember</h3>
<p>Please do not click on the same card twice. That is not the purpose of the game!!</p>
